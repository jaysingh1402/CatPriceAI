EXCESS OF LOSS REINSURANCE AGREEMENT
BETWEEN GULF COAST INSURANCE COMPANY (CEDANT)
AND ATLAS REINSURANCE LIMITED (REINSURER)

TREATY NO: TRT-2024-001
INCEPTION: January 1, 2024
EXPIRY:    December 31, 2024

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 1 — COVERAGE AND STRUCTURE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 This Agreement constitutes a Catastrophe Excess of Loss (Cat XL)
    reinsurance contract. The Reinsurer agrees to indemnify the Cedant
    for Ultimate Net Loss (UNL) in excess of the Cedant's Retention,
    subject to the Reinsurer's Limit of Liability.

1.2 LAYER STRUCTURE:
    Retention (Attachment Point): USD 50,000,000 per Occurrence
    Limit of Liability:           USD 50,000,000 per Occurrence
    Exhaustion Point:             USD 100,000,000 per Occurrence

1.3 The Reinsurer's liability in any one Occurrence shall not exceed
    USD 50,000,000 in any one Occurrence and USD 100,000,000 in
    the aggregate in any one Contract Year.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 2 — PERILS COVERED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

2.1 This Agreement covers losses arising from the following perils:
    (a) Named Windstorm (Hurricane, Tropical Storm, Typhoon)
    (b) Flood (including Storm Surge and Inland Flood)
    (c) Tornado and Hail (where part of a qualifying Occurrence)

2.2 The following perils are EXCLUDED:
    (a) Earthquake and Earthquake Fire Following
    (b) Terrorism and Cyber Events
    (c) Nuclear, Radiological, Biological, Chemical (NRBC)
    (d) War and Military Action
    (e) Pandemic and Communicable Disease
    (f) Flood arising from levee failure NOT caused by a named storm

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 3 — TERRITORY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 This Agreement applies to direct insurance policies written by
    the Cedant covering risks located in the following US States:
    Texas (TX), Louisiana (LA), Mississippi (MS), Alabama (AL),
    Florida (FL).

3.2 Offshore platforms and marine risks are EXCLUDED.

3.3 Risks located outside the Territory are EXCLUDED even if
    the Cedant's policy covers those risks.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 4 — REINSTATEMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 Following any loss payment, the Reinsurer's limit shall be
    automatically reinstated up to TWO (2) times during the
    Contract Year.

4.2 Reinstatement Premium:
    - First Reinstatement: 100% of the pro-rata annual premium
    - Second Reinstatement: 100% of the pro-rata annual premium

4.3 Reinstatement premium shall be payable within 30 days of the
    loss payment triggering the reinstatement.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 5 — DEFINITIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 OCCURRENCE: A single catastrophic event, including all losses
    arising within a 168-hour (7 calendar day) window for windstorm
    and flood perils. The Cedant selects the most favorable window.

5.2 ULTIMATE NET LOSS (UNL): The actual loss paid by the Cedant
    after recoveries from all inuring recoveries (e.g., primary
    reinsurance, retrocession), but before this Agreement.

5.3 RATE ON LINE (ROL): The annual reinsurance premium expressed
    as a percentage of the layer limit. ROL = Premium / Limit.

5.4 ATTACHMENT POINT: The threshold above which the Reinsurer
    begins to pay. The Cedant retains all losses up to this amount.

5.5 BURNING COST: The historical average annual loss to the layer,
    computed as: Total layer losses / Number of experience years.

5.6 IBNR (Incurred But Not Reported): Loss reserves for claims
    that have occurred but have not yet been reported to the Cedant.

5.7 DEMAND SURGE: The increase in claims cost following a major
    catastrophe due to temporary shortage of materials and labor.
    This is a recognized component of Cat loss development.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 6 — PREMIUM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

6.1 The Deposit Premium (initial estimate) shall be:
    Provisional Rate on Line × Layer Limit = Deposit Premium
    Subject to adjustment based on final exposure submission.

6.2 The Adjustment Premium will be calculated at year-end based
    on the Cedant's actual subject premium written.

6.3 Minimum Premium: 75% of the Deposit Premium.

6.4 Payment: Premium is payable quarterly in arrears.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 7 — LOSS REPORTING AND CLAIMS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

7.1 The Cedant shall notify the Reinsurer of any Occurrence likely
    to generate a loss under this Agreement as soon as practicable,
    and in any event within 30 days of the Cedant becoming aware.

7.2 Proof of Loss to be submitted within 90 days of final loss
    determination, including:
    (a) Itemized loss bordereaux
    (b) Actuarial development summary
    (c) Statement of IBNR reserves

7.3 The Reinsurer has the right to inspect all records related
    to any loss claimed under this Agreement.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 8 — LOSS SETTLEMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

8.1 The Reinsurer shall pay its share of losses within 45 days
    of receipt of satisfactory proof of loss.

8.2 Interim loss payments may be made for large losses where
    the Cedant provides satisfactory interim loss estimates.

8.3 FOLLOW THE FORTUNES: The Reinsurer agrees to follow the
    fortunes of the Cedant with respect to all loss settlements
    made by the Cedant in good faith, subject to the terms
    of this Agreement.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ARTICLE 9 — RETROCESSION AND OTHER RECOVERIES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

9.1 This Agreement sits above all INURING recoveries, meaning
    the Cedant must first exhaust any prior-layer reinsurance
    before claiming under this Agreement.

9.2 Subrogation: Any subrogation recoveries shall be shared
    pro-rata between the Cedant and the Reinsurer in proportion
    to their respective shares of the original loss.

ARTICLE 10 — ARBITRATION AND GOVERNING LAW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

10.1 Any dispute shall first be submitted to the parties' senior
     management for resolution.

10.2 Unresolved disputes shall be referred to Arbitration under
     the ARIAS-U.S. Procedures for Insured-Reinsurer Disputes.

10.3 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State
     of New York.
